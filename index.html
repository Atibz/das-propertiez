Frequently Asked Questions

Can I buy property in the UK if I don’t live there?

Yes, non-residents can legally purchase property in both the UK. Our team guides you through all the legal and financial requirements, no matter where you live.

Do I need a visa or residency to invest in property abroad?

No, you don’t need a visa or residency status to own property in the UK. However, each country has unique rules for foreign buyers, which we help you navigate.

What documents do I need to buy property?

Typical documents include:

  • International passport
  • Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement)
  • Source of funds declaration
  • Bank reference or financial profile
  • Any relevant tax documentation (varies by country)

We’ll provide a full checklist based on your location and chosen country.

Can I get a mortgage as a foreign buyer?

Yes, in many cases you can. While we don’t provide mortgages directly, we can introduce you to trusted UK-based mortgage companies and brokers who specialize in working with international buyers. They'll guide you through the eligibility and application process.

What’s the buying process like for international buyers?

It generally includes:

  • Property selection and offer
  • Reservation and legal checks
  • Payment (or mortgage process)
  • Property handover

We handle the entire process from start to finish virtually or in-person.

What are the hidden or ongoing costs I should know about?

Common costs include:

  • Legal fees
  • Stamp duty or property tax
  • Maintenance fees (for apartments)
  • Currency conversion or transfer fees

We’ll break these down for each property you’re interested in.

Do you offer remote services for international clients?

Absolutely. Our services include virtual property tours, online documentation, and dedicated consultants who guide you whether you're in Dubai, New York, Nairobi, Lagos, or anywhere else.

What kind of properties can I invest in?

We offer:

  • Residential apartments and homes
  • Investment flats with guaranteed rental income
  • Off-plan developments
  • Vacation Rentals & Short-Term Lets
  • Commercial properties (on request)
Can you help manage my property after purchase?

Yes. We offer property management services — from tenant sourcing and rent collection to maintenance and reporting.

What if I want to resell or rent my property later?

We support resale, property valuation, and helping you list on rental platforms or through our network.

How do I get started?

Simply contact us via our website or WhatsApp. We’ll discuss your goals, budget, and location preference, then recommend matching options.

Can I get personalized advice before I buy?

Absolutely. We offer free consultations for all first-time and returning buyers.
